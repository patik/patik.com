Machu Picchu

Machu Picchu (Quechua: Machu Pikchu, "Old Mountain") is a pre-Columbian 15th-century Inca site located 7,970 ft above sea level. It is situated on a mountain ridge above the Urubamba Valley in Peru, northwest of Cusco.

La Bombonera

The Estadio Alberto J. Armando is a stadium located in the La Boca district of Buenos Aires. Widely known as La Bombonera (the Chocolate Box) due to its shape, it is the home of Boca Juniors, one of Argentina's most famous football clubs.
